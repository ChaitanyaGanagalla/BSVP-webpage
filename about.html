Research Focus

      
Dr. BSVP specializes in the development of tools for fluid-structure-interaction (FSI) in Fluid-Thermal systems. His group has worked on wide spectrum of FSI problems ranging from wakes past bluff bodies to cardio-vascular simulations. They use SPH, DPD methods, and study CHF margins in rod bundle heat transfer.
